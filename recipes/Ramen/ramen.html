Cheesy Ramen Noodles


Description

Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup with thousands of variations.

Ramen noodles are not inherently healthy or unhealthy, but they provide limited nutritional value on their own. They contain carbohydrates, fats, protein and some micronutrients like B vitamins and iron.

Ingredients
  • 2 cups water
  • 1(3 ounce) package any flavor ramen noodles
  • 1 slice American cheese
Steps
  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender, about 2 minutes.
  3. Pour out water, then stir in seasoning packet and cheese until well blended.
